Projects

Some of the projects we have worked on include:

Customer Central

Appleconn cc operates a dedicated Customer Central operation based in Fourways, Johannesburg. This function has adopted a customer strategy that has enabled companies to face their ever-spiralling costs in a completely different way. This concept is called KAS (Key Account Support), which provides the following services to its customers:

  • Dedicated local call rate telephone number
  • Order entry and processing
  • One focal point to harness the power of Appleconn ( stock availability)
  • Extended opening hours 7am to 6pm weekdays
  • 24x365 emergency call out service
  • Direct marketing activities

This service provision provides many of the benefits that our customers require namely:

  • Consistent service response
  • Consistent pricing
  • Adherence to customers instructions
  • Access to numerous stock lines
  • Compliance with order entry requirements
  • Reduction in the number of vendors

Smarter Solutions

Appleconn has been a pioneer in developing and improving e-business service for customers, helping them to save considerable amounts every year through smarter supply chain solutions. The following ranges of options are available:

  • Full range of EDI and e-procurement services
  • Alliances with e-technology partners and specialist procurement communities to provide web-catalogues and integrated EDI solutions

Account Management

Appleconn cc provides as part of the trading package an opportunity to provide the following services which are designed to reduce administration:

  • Consignment Stocks – Automatically replenished stock profiles held at your own premises. Stock is reviewed weekly by our staff and replenished as per your requirements
  • E-Business and Electronic Data Interchange – E-Business offers the potential to enhance the efficiency of procurement processes thus reducing costs for all those in the supply chain. We have developed a number of standard and bespoke e-trading opportunities for our customers and suppliers using web technology, EDI solutions and electronic market places. A dedicated team of e-business specialists supports these initiatives
  • Project Management – Bestpoken arrangements to ensure that products are sourced and supplied on time and in line with construction schedules allowing customers to concentrate on the other complex areas of major projects
